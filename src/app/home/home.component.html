About The Confrence

ICICS is a series of conferences initiated by School of Electronics and Electrical Engineering at Lovely Professional University. The 2nd International Conference on Intelligent Circuits and Systems (ICICS 2018) will explore the new horizons of innovations of researchers working for the development of smart and green technologies in the field of Energy, Electronics, Communication, Computers and Control. ICICS always aims to identify new opportunities for the social and economic benefits of the society. ICICS builds a bridge between academics and R & D institutions, social visionaries and experts from all strata of society to present the on-going research activities and foster research relations between them. It provides opportunities to:

done exchange new ideas, applications and experiences

done define the path forward to establish research relations

done find global partners for future collaboration.
